Why Join DDSR?

Here's a few great reasons to become a member:

  • Runners receive automatic membership in the Road Runners Club of America (RRCA), which includes insurance coverage at all races. This only applies to those who state they are runners on the application.
  • Cyclists (all members in the club) will receive insurance coverage for club rides through the League of American Bicyclists (LAB).
  • Get discount membership to League of American Bicyclists.
  • Meet with others in the community who enjoy running and cycling.
  • Ability to cross train or partner with other athletes for duathlons and triathlons.
  • Improve your overall fitness and running or cycling performance by working with others to achieve your personal fitness goals.
  • Support the running and cycling community by volunteering at local events.
  • Car pool with members to regional races and rides.
  • Holiday and Summer parties, and breakfast runs throughout the year.
  • Volunteer opportunties at local races and our Adpot a Highway cleaning
