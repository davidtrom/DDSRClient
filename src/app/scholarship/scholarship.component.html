The DDSR Memorial Scholarship

2020 DDSR Memorial Scholarship Guidelines:

The Downstate Delaware Striders and Riders are pleased to announce that the club will be awarding scholarships again in 2020.
There will be three (3) $500 DOWNSTATE DELAWARE STRIDERS AND RIDERS MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIPS.
The deadline for the scholarship application is April 30, 2020.

Eligibility for a Scholarship:

  • Graduating from a Kent County Delaware high school.
  • A member of a cross country or track team for at least two years.
  • GPA of 2.75 or higher.
  • Accepted at a two-year or four-year college.
  • Verification of the above four items.
  • Letter of recommendation from your coach or a teacher.
  • A short essay, which is described in further detail below.
  • A cover letter which includes all the items in the Checklist below.

Essay:

Your essay should be 500 words or less. The essay topic that must be addressed is "How did physical fitness enhance your educational experience, and how do you plan to incorporate physical fitness into your life as you move forward into the future."

Checklist of Items

A cover letter with the following:
  • Name.
  • Address.
  • Phone Number.
  • Email Address.
  • High School.
  • GPA.
  • College or University admitted to.
  • Name of coach(es).
In addition:
  • Verification of GPA.
  • Letter of recommendation from your coach or a teacher.
  • Your Essay.
  • Copy of acceptance letter to College or University.
