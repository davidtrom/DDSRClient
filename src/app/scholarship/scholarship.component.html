The DDSR Memorial Scholarship

2020 DDSR Memorial Scholarship Guidelines:

The Downstate Delaware Striders and Riders are pleased to announce that the club will be awarding scholarships again in 2020.
There will be three (3) $500 DOWNSTATE DELAWARE STRIDERS AND RIDERS MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIPS
The deadline for the scholarship application is April 30, 2020.

Eligibility for the Scholarship:

  • Graduating from a Kent County Delaware high school.
  • A member of a cross country or track team for at least two years.
  • GPA of 2.75 or higher.
  • Accepted at a two-year or four-year college.
  • Verification of the above four items.
  • Letter of recommendation from your coach or teacher.
  • A short essay, which is described in further detail below.
