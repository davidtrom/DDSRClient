Interested in Particpating in Local Races?

From north to south and in between, if you're looking for a race to test your fitness, Delaware has a race for you. You can choose race distances from 1 mile to full and ultra marathons. There are races at the beach, in the cities, and on the trails of our state parks. Check out the links below for a race near you!

Central Delaware

If you're local to the central Delaware area, rest assured TriSports Charitable Events has a race for you!

Northern Delaware and the Beaches

For events primarly held at the beach or in the northern Delaware area, Races2Run has you covered.

Southern Delaware and the Beaches

For races almost exclusively in Southern Delaware and the beach areas, and utilizing some of the wonderful Delaware State park trails, look no farther than the Seashore Striders.

Local, Regional, National

Not sure exactly what you're looking for? Looking for a race to run in another state or while you're away on vacation? RunningInTheUSA is your one stop shop to find races of all distances and surfaces, even multi-sport events.
