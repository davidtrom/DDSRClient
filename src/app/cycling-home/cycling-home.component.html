Partner Groups & Clubs in the Community

Bike Delaware advocates for safe, convenient and fun bicycling and walking for everyone. Their vision is that people of all ages, abilities and means choose to bicycle and walk for health, work and play. Delaware communities are connected by a bicycle-friendly transportation network, where all users interact safely and respectfully.
White Clay Bicycle Club was established in 1973, the White Clay Bicycle Club (WCBC) consists of over 400 cyclists from Delaware, Maryland and Pennsylvania. We come in all shapes and sizes, young and old, and all abilities with one very important factor in common—a love of cycling! We are “roadies” and tourists, racers, mountain and tandem bikers, commuters and daily riders, weekend and occasional riders, and even non-riders. What keeps all of us going is our enthusiasm for health, camaraderie, the love of the outdoors and a desire to learn more about ourselves and our sport.
